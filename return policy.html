Return Policy

Home /Return Policy

Overview

We hope you are 100% satisfied with your Stepify.com purchase. If for any reason you are not completely satisfied, Stepify offers a refund or exchange on unopened and unused, full-priced items within 30 days after your purchase date. To set up a return, please start your return HERE.

At this time, items ordered online cannot be returned in the Stepify Fashion Island store.

Damages

If upon arrival, any part of your order is damaged, please email @stepify.com as soon as possible and include your order number and a picture of the damage. Any inquiry must be received by Stepify within 30 days of the purchase date. note: Once product is removed from its original protective packaging, we cannot be responsible for any further damage due to improper handling.

Sale Items

All sale products are final sale and are not eligible for returns or exchanges. Online discounts and promotional codes cannot be combined with sale items.

Custom Gift Sets

All custom gift sets are final sale and are not eligible for returns or exchanges.

Bulk Orders

orders that have been pre-approved are made to order and are final sale. Bulk Orders are not eligible for returns or exchanges. Bulk discounts are not valid on sale items.
