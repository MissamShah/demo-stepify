ORDER PROCESSING & TRANSIT TIMES

Typical order processing times are between 1-2 business days. Ground Shipping will take anywhere from 1-6 business days, based on location. Packages are shipped via FEDEX or USPS, and typical transit times by region are as follows:
1 - 2 Days: California + Southwestern states
3 - 4 Days: Texas, Pacific Northwest + Some Midwestern states
4 - 6 Days: Southern + Northeastern states
