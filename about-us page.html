WHO WE ARE & WHAT WE DO

About Us

Stepify was founded in the UK in 1989 and is entirely owned by the Saleem Family. With over 33 years of experience in the footwear industry, Stepify has grown through a passion and dedication to excellence in product development. We have a strong presence in the UK and Pakistan and are focusing on both retail and online expansion. Our brand continues to lead as the stylish, chic, and smart destination for trend-right footwear and accessories for contemporary men, women, and kids leading multi-faceted lifestyles. We offer confidence and versatility for work, weekend, and going-out occasions. Our styles are elegant, feminine, glamorous, and sophisticated, using the finest quality materials and intricate handwork by our skilled craftsmen. Our shoes and bags are showcased in vibrant colors combined with luxurious fabrics.

What We Offer:

  • High Quality Men’s, Women’s, & Kids’ Footwear
  • Lifetime Refund Policy
  • Serving Millions of Happy Customers Worldwide and Still Counting!
  • Excellent Delivery Service

Our Timeline

  • 1989 – The idea for a global brand came into existence in 1989 in London. The company initially focused on retail and has since developed a substantial wholesale business and a versatile product range, supplying numerous retailers throughout the UK and the rest of the world.
  • 1990 – Our first store opened in 1990 on Green Street, which is the 4th most popular commercial street in London.
  • 1992 – Due to growing demand and to gain market share within the UK, a second store was opened in Whitechapel.
  • 1999 – We launched our very first online store, considered the most stylish online footwear store in the UK.
  • 2000 – We entrusted the brand to others and launched the wholesale business in Europe and the rest of the world.
  • 2003 – Stepify London opened its third store on Green Street to meet customer demand and showcase the vast range of styles produced by the company.
  • 2006 – This was a busy year. We opened stores in Sheffield, Leeds, and Chelmsford. Starting in March 2006, Stepify was poised to open 20 other franchises. The sheer popularity and appeal of the Stepify brand led to inquiries from all around the world, from the USA and South Africa to Australia and Asia. The brand proved to be renowned within the UK and globally.
  • 2018 & 2019 – Stepify London opened 9 flagship stores in Pakistan: 1. Packages Mall Mega Store, Lahore 2. Emporium Mall, Lahore 3. Jhang Store 4. Multan Store 5. Peshawar Store 6. Amanah Mall 7. Islamabad Store 8. Faisalabad Store 9. Sialkot Store, and is planning to expand further throughout Pakistan. It remains a key global player in men’s and women’s fashion footwear. More customers than ever before embraced pieces from Stepify London’s SS 17 Collection and experienced the unique appeal of the Stepify brand.
  • 2020 – Stepify opened 17 stores across Pakistan.
